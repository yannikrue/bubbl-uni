bubble.uni

bubble.uni is an agar.io inspired real-time game where up to four players each control a circle-shaped player character with the goal to reach the maximum size before the other players and before the match ends. To accomplish this goal, the players can collect so called energizers and fire bubbles at their opponents to take away some mass from them.

A: move to the left
S: move down
D: move to the right
Left mouse button: fire bubble
